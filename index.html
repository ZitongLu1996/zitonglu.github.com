              <h3 style="color: black">About Me!</h3>

              <p style="font-size: medium">I'm now a master student in cognitive neuroscience from East China Normal University (Shanghai, China) and involved in Memory, Attention and Cognition Lab advised by Professor Yixuan Ku. My research combines behavioral, EEG, fMRI and computational models to better understand the cognitive processes of visual working memory and attention. I'm particularly interested in using some novel methods such as machine learning, deep learning and representational similarity analysis to decode memory and further explore the dynamic representation of memory in our brain. In addition, I'm the author of <a href="https://zitonglu1996.github.io/NeuroRA/">NeuroRA</a> (a Python toolbox of Representational Analysis).
